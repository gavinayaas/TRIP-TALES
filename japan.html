Top Tourist Destinations

Tokyo Tower

Iconic red-and-white tower offering panoramic views of Tokyo.

Mount Fuji

Japan's tallest mountain and a symbol of natural beauty.

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Famous shrine with thousands of red torii gates in Kyoto.

Shibuya Crossing

The bustling pedestrian crossing in the heart of Tokyo.

Popular Japanese Food

Sushi

Delicious fresh fish and rice rolls, a classic Japanese delicacy.

Ramen

Warm, flavorful noodle soup with broth, meat, and veggies.

Takoyaki

Fried octopus balls, a famous street food in Osaka.

Matcha Dessert

Green tea flavored sweets including cakes, ice cream, and mochi.
